Nakumbuka siku, vicheko na tabasamu,
Tukitembea pamoja, tulipotea katika upendo.
Kisha ikaja dhoruba, bila kutarajia,
Ikatutenganisha, ikaacha mioyo yetu katika maumivu na machafuko.

Furaha yangu, my joy, my light,
Lost in the dark, but now we reunite.
Furaha yangu, you’re my guiding star,
Back in my arms, where you belong.

Muda ulipita, na kumbukumbu zikibaki,
Kila kona, kila ndoto, niliuona uso wako.
Kupitia majaribu, machozi, na tamaa,
Nilishikilia matumaini, upendo wetu ungepata nafasi yake.

Furaha yangu, my joy, my light,
Lost in the dark, but now we reunite.
Furaha yangu, you’re my guiding star,
Back in my arms, where you belong.

Sasa tumesimama pamoja, imara kuliko awali,
Mioyo imepona, majeraha yamepona, upendo umerudi.
Hakuna kivuli tena, hakuna hofu,
Ni upendo tu, ni furaha tu, ni machozi ya furaha tu.

Furaha yangu, my joy, my light,
Lost in the dark, but now we reunite.
Furaha yangu, you’re my guiding star,
Back in my arms, where you belong.

Furaha yangu, upendo wetu hautafifia kamwe,
Katika mikono yako, milele nitakaa.
Furaha yangu, tuko pamoja tena,
Katika amani, katika upendo, milele.
